Garbage & Solid Waste — Why it matters

Uncollected garbage reduces quality of life, spreads disease, and blocks drains — timely action prevents public health crises.

Overview

Improper disposal and delayed collection of solid waste cause foul odour, attract pests, and contaminate soil and groundwater.

Consequences of Neglecting Garbage

  • Increased vector-borne diseases (mosquitoes, rats)
  • Blocked drains causing waterlogging and flood risk
  • Air pollution and respiratory problems from burning waste
  • Decline in neighbourhood aesthetics & property values

What Citizens Can Do

  • Report overflowing bins and illegal dumping with photos
  • Use designated collection timings and segregate waste
  • Encourage community cleanliness drives and awareness

How SevaSetu Helps

File a complaint with photos and location — SevaSetu routes it to the Sanitation department and tracks SLA. AI prioritizes hotspots needing immediate action.
