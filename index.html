Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about filing complaints, tracking, AI prioritization, privacy and more.

Sign in (or continue as guest if enabled), choose the correct department, describe the issue, attach photos or video, and submit. You’ll receive a unique token to track the complaint.

Include exact location (landmark/street), a short clear description, date/time, and photos or short videos showing the problem. Evidence speeds up verification and action.

Our models analyse the complaint text and attached images to detect category, severity, hazards (e.g., exposed wires, deep potholes), and sentiment (urgent vs routine). The system then suggests a priority so officers can address the most critical issues first.

Time-to-resolution varies by department and priority. After submission you can track the complaint status using your token. High priority/urgent issues are routed to immediate action where possible; follow-up timelines appear in the complaint details.

You can file complaints as a registered user for better tracking and history. Some jurisdictions may also allow guest submissions — check the form. Registered users get notifications and a history of previous complaints.

Use the complaint page to request escalation or add comments. If unresolved, follow the escalation path shown in complaint details (escalation to senior officer / grievance cell). You may also contact support via the helpline listed in the footer.

SmartSeva follows data protection practices: personal data is used only for complaint processing and communication. Uploaded media is stored securely and shared only with relevant departments. For details see the Privacy policy link in the footer.

SmartSeva supports multiple Indian languages (English, हिंदी, తెలుగు, தமிழ் — and more as added). Use the language selector in the header to switch the UI. Complaints written in local languages will be processed and translated where needed for routing and AI analysis.

We accept common image formats (JPEG/PNG) and short videos (MP4/WebM). Recommended max file size is listed on the upload form — compress large files before upload. Avoid uploading sensitive personal information in images.

For technical issues or accessibility assistance, email support@SmartSeva.gov or call the helpline number listed in the footer. Provide the token or screenshot to help us troubleshoot faster.
