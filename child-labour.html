Child Labour — Protecting Children

Reports about children working inform social services and protect rights.

Raise Complaint

Overview

Child labour denies children education, harms health and perpetuates poverty cycles.

Consequences of Neglect

  • Loss of schooling and reduced lifetime earnings
  • Physical & mental health problems for children
  • Intergenerational poverty and exploitation
If you see a child working: report location, age estimate, and employer details if known. Immediate action can prevent harm.

How SevaSetu Helps

Your report goes to social welfare and labour departments; child protection teams are engaged and follow-up tracked.
