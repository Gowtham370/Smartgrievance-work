Overview

Power line faults, fallen poles, and exposed wiring pose safety and reliability risks.

Consequences of Neglect

  • Electrocution and fatal injuries
  • Fires and property damage
  • Longer outages impacting health-care and businesses
Urgent: If you see a live exposed wire, keep clear, warn others, and report immediately.

How SevaSetu Helps

Reports are sent to the Electricity Department / Discom and marked as high priority by AI when hazards are detected.
